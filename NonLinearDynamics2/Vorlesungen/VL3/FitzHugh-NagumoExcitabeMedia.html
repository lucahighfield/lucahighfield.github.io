Excitable Media: FitzHugh-Nagumo Model

Interactive visualization for Figure 10 of the script. A supercritical perturbation (u ↑) leads to a large excursion in phase space (A → B → C → D → O).

Parameters & Controls

Values < 0.3 are subcritical. The system immediately returns to O.

Controls how fast the inhibitor v reacts. Small ε means sharp u-pulses.

Observation Tip: Click on "Trigger perturbation". In the phase space on the right, you'll see the trajectory jump up from O. If the perturbation is strong enough, it crosses the middle nullcline (dashed curve) and the system must take the long detour over the right branch (excitation).
